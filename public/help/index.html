Main Rules (Help)

The object of the game is to become the wealthiest player through buying, renting and selling of property.

The equipment consists of a board, 2 dice, tokens, 32 houses and 12 Hotels. There are 16 Chance and 16 Community Chest cards, 28 Title Deed card (one for each property), and play money. (Each player is given $1500 divided)

Whenever you land on an unowned property you may buy that property from the Bank at its printed price. You receive the Title Deed card showing ownership. When you land on a property that is owned by another player, the owner collects rent from you in accordance with the list printed on its Title Deed card.

Chances and Community Chests are cards which have some mission and you have to complete them

Whenever you land on an another's property you must pay rent: 1house=1.5*initial rent 2house=1house*1.5 3house=2house*1.5 4house=3house*1.5 and 1hotel=4house*2 (You can add houses and hotels only your turn).

Whenever you can't pay the rent, tax or do the chance cards mission you lose.
